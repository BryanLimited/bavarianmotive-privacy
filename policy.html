Privacy Policy for BLink

Last updated: December 22, 2026

BLink is a BMW-focused vehicle diagnostics application developed by BavarianMotive.

Information We Collect

  • Email address for authentication (Firebase Auth)

  • Improve performance, reliability, and safety

We do not sell or rent personal data. Data may be processed by trusted service providers such as Firebase and OpenAI solely to provide app functionality.

Data Security

Industry-standard security practices are used. Sensitive credentials and API keys are stored securely and never embedded in the app.
